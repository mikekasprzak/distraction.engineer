## Leviathan Wakes by James S.A. Corey (The Expanse: Book 1)
The Expanse television series just ended, and it covers only until the end of Book 6 (of 9).

Shit, I guess we're reading now.

Jenna made an effort to point out differences between the book an the show as we watched, but my memory is trash, so I'm best off reading from the beginning. I mean I feel I was _told_ to read from the beginning in [few words](https://twitter.com/jennaleedoyle/status/1481798766969724930). Also we named our cat Avasarala after Shohreh Aghdashloo's character from the show. As I write this, cat sits perched behind me staring, so between the two of them (Jenna and cat) I may have been peer pressured into reading the series.

So alright, lets begin our first book of 2022.
