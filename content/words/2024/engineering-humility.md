Arrogantly I never think that I'm arrogant, but everyone is. Arrogance is a tool, the practice of hiding or burying anxieties to appear confident. Socially you must exude confidence to be trusted, and you need trust. Easier said than done when you're an over-thinker.

Two years ago when I rebooted my reboot-blog's reboot's reboot (AKA `TooNormal`), I chose to title it `The.Distraction.Engineer`. While I really like the `Distraction.Engineer` identity, and despite how well it summarizes my interests and accomplishments, something about it as my blog title feels arrogant, like I haven't earned it. I'm keeping the name, but for less friction, I'd like to call my blog something else.

This blog is now `Distraction.Engineering`.
