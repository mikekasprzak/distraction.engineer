This is Mike's place. Mike makes things. Mike entertains. Mike engineers distractions.

Mike pays the bills creating software and offering services with his company. On the side he writes, makes videos, and dabbles in other creative pursuits.

Mike is a developer. He was a game industry professional from 1999, shipping numerous games for PC, Mobile, PlayStation, and GameBoy over a span of 20-some years. He's still involved in games as the organizer and caretaker of the [Ludum Dare](https://ludumdare.com) online game jam event.

Mike owns and runs [Interactive Snacks Limited](https://interactivesnacks.com), a software company tasked with supporting Ludum Dare, among other things.

This website is his new online home. Part blog, part portfolio.

May your stay be filled with distraction.
